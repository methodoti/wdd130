History

Founded in 1998, Rapids Whitewater Rafting began as a small family venture in Salmon, Idaho. Passionate about adventure, we aimed to share the thrill of the river with everyone.

Today, we've grown into a trusted name, guiding thousands through Idaho's wild rapids while prioritizing safety and unforgettable experiences.
